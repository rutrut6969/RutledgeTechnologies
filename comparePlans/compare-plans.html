FAQs

How long does a site take?

Starter/Professional typically 1–3 weeks depending on content readiness. Advanced may take 3–4 weeks. All-Inclusive/Partner operate continuously with monthly improvements.

Who owns the site?

You own the finished site after final payment (Starter/Pro/Advanced). For All-Inclusive/Partner, ownership transfers after the 12-month term or earlier buyout per the contract.

Can I upgrade plans later?

Yes—credits and migrations are available. We’ll recommend the smoothest path based on your current setup.

What’s included vs. an add-on?

Add-ons include e-commerce, additional integrations, more pages, and custom features. We’ll quote add-ons during scoping.

Can I cancel early?

All-Inclusive/Partner include an early termination buyout. See the contract for exact terms.
